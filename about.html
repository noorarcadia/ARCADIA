About ARCADIA:

Founded in 2023, Arcadia is the first ever pakistan dance blog on the web.It’s written and produced by beginner dancer(stage name) Arcadia and covers the essential dancers, crews, performances, and ideas that increase the collective adjacent possible of humanity. Frequent topics of interest include different styles of dance crew updates, kpop events happening in all over Pakistan, some of the dance studios in karachi , design, music and carefully curated current events, all of it lightly contextualized. Basically, it’s the pakistan's and specially karachi's complete knowledge with all the advantages and disadvantages that entails.
